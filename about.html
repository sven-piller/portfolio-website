About Sven

I’m an aviation expert based at Frankfurt/Main, Germany. I have been working since 2010 on UX Design and rapid prototyping and have built multiple mobile apps which are in productive use at Lufthansa. A focus lays on real-time applications which are used in busses, tractors and service companions for VIPs and children.

Last year there was the chance to leverage my knowledge from an autodidact level to an expert with the UX Design course from careerfoundry.com
