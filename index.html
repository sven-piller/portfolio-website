LEOS Crewbus

With this hybrid web-app the Lufthansa crews are able to get the information whether a crew bus is coming for their arrival flight. They only have to enter their airline and flight number.
At the moment this service is only available for flights at Frankfurt.

SNOWHUNT

If you want to do more than ordinary winter sports SNOWHUNT is the app for you. You can chose of several scavenger hunts near your actual location and fulfill tasks like multiple choice questions, photo contests or riding patterns on the slope.

LEOS Tow Tractor Client

Tow drivers at Frankfurt and Duesseldorf get their actual tasks and updates on their devices. In the other direction the disposition see if the tow driver changes their state, e.g. from towing to finished, or get information if there are any disturbances at the taxiway like ambulances.
